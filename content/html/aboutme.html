A little bit about me

My name is Holden Wiese and I was born and raised in Sandy, Utah. My family has always been important to me and even today, my brother and sister are two of the best friends I have.

I worked hard in highschool and spent my freetime with friends snowboarding, skateboarding, and rock climbing. I went to Utah State University for a year where I completed most of my general courses and from there, I went to work at a computer store where I learned a lot and developed a passion for computers.

After a year I decided to serve and ecclesiastical mission. I went to Virginia and North Carolina and spent 2 years there teaching and giving service to the people. After returning home I completed my remaining general courses at Utah Valley University and graduated a Web Development Bootcamp with The Tech Academy.

Currently, I am working at a rock climbing gym in Salt Lake City and studying in pursuit of a career in programming.
